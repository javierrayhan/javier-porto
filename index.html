DIY Heat Plate

The Heat Plate Controller OLED/16x2 I2C is a microcontroller-based temperature controller using a relay and thermistor, with real-time display via OLED or 16x2 I2C LCD. It allows temperature adjustment with a rotary encoder and automatically controls heat, perfect for DIY soldering stations or compact heating devices.
